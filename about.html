About the artist

Richard Oldfield

Art by Richard Oldfield. A collection of my works in watercolour, acrylic and oils. Inspired by the world around me.

About me

I began my current art journey when I retired. I have been inspired and supported by a number of artists and teachers who have helped me to develop my skills. I have not settled on a particular style or medium, but work mostly in watercolour.

I particularly enjoy being outside and sketching, mostly city and urban scenes. I take lots of photographs when I am out and about and on holiday, and use these and my sketches to inspire the work back in my little studio.

Many of the pieces on the site are for sale and details are available on the gallery pages or sales page.
