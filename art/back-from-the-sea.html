About this painting

Back from the Sea captures the quiet charm of a traditional fishing boat moored after its journey. The subject reflects the rhythm of coastal life and the character of Greece’s island harbours.

It is a piece for anyone drawn to boats, Greek islands, travel memories and peaceful maritime scenes.
