About this painting

Rendezvous suggests a moment of anticipation. The figure, umbrella and rainy night setting give the painting a cinematic quality, inviting the viewer to imagine the story behind the scene.

It is a piece for anyone drawn to atmospheric figurative work, rainy city scenes, mystery and paintings with a narrative quality.
