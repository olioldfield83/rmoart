About this painting

Hilbre Island No2 focuses on the island’s distinctive red cliffs, shoreline and houses above the coast. The painting captures the familiar character of this special coastal location.

It is a piece for anyone connected to Hilbre Island, the Wirral coast or seascapes with strong colour and local identity.
