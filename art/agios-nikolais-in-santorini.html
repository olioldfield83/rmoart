About this painting

Agios Nikolais in Santorini is inspired by the island’s striking architecture, intense light and memorable views. The painting reflects the contrast of white buildings, warm atmosphere and the sense of place that makes Santorini so distinctive.

It is a piece for anyone drawn to Greece, travel, island architecture and paintings that capture the feeling of a memorable location.
