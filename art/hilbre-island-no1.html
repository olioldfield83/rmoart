About this painting

Hilbre Island No1 captures the character of this distinctive coastal location, with houses sitting above the cliff and the sea air shaping the atmosphere of the scene.

It is a piece for anyone drawn to coastal landscapes, island views and familiar Wirral places captured in watercolour.
