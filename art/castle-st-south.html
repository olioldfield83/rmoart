About this painting

Castle St South reflects Richard’s interest in buildings, streets and urban scenes. The painting focuses on the strength of the architecture and the visual rhythm of the street.

It is a piece for anyone drawn to urban watercolours, architectural subjects and familiar places captured with atmosphere and character.
