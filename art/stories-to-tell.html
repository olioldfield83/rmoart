About this painting

Stories to Tell is about the character found in older buildings. Doorways, windows and weathered surfaces often suggest lives, places and memories beyond what is immediately visible.

It is a piece for anyone drawn to architectural detail, travel memories, old buildings and paintings with a quiet narrative quality.
