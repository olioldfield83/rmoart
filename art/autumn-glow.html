About this painting

Autumn Glow celebrates the beauty of the changing season. The pathway gives the painting a sense of direction and calm, while the surrounding autumn colours create warmth and atmosphere.

It is a piece for anyone drawn to woodland walks, seasonal colour and the quiet magic of autumn landscapes.
