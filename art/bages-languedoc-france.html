About this sketch

Bages, Languedoc, France reflects Richard’s interest in sketching places while travelling. Urban sketching captures not only the buildings and streets, but also the feeling of being there.

This piece is ideal for anyone drawn to French villages, travel sketches, architectural detail and artwork that preserves the memory of a particular place.
