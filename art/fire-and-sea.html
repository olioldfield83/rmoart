About this painting

Fire and Sea captures the presence and drama of Liverpool’s waterfront. The Liver Bird stands proudly over the scene, giving the painting a strong sense of place and identity.

It is a piece for anyone connected to Liverpool, drawn to waterfront architecture, or interested in city scenes with atmosphere and character.
