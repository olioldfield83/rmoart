About this painting

Lookout Tower is inspired by the architecture and atmosphere of Pals in Catalunya. The tower gives the painting a strong focal point, while the surrounding forms suggest the texture and history of the place.

It is a piece for anyone drawn to travel, architectural subjects, Mediterranean towns and paintings that capture a memorable location.
