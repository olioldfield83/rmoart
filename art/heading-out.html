About this painting

Heading Out captures the moment of departure: a single sailboat moving away from shore and into open water. The simplicity of the subject gives the painting a quiet, reflective mood.

It is a piece for anyone drawn to sailing, coastal views, open water and the peaceful atmosphere of a boat setting out to sea.
