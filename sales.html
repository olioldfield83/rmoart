Commission artwork

Richard is available for selected commission work, including portraits, pet portraits, buildings, landscapes, urban sketches, places with personal meaning and special gifts.

To discuss a commission, please email Richard directly. You are welcome to attach reference photographs to your email.

Commission enquiries:
richard@rmoart.co.uk

Email Richard about a commission

What to include in your email

  • What you would like commissioned, such as a portrait, pet portrait, building, landscape or urban sketch
  • Any reference photographs you would like Richard to use
  • Your preferred medium, such as watercolour, pen and wash, oil, acrylic or pencil
  • Your preferred size, such as A5, A4, A3, A2 or a custom size
  • Whether you would like the artwork framed or unframed
  • Your approximate budget
  • Any deadline or special date
  • Your location, especially if collection or delivery may be needed

Richard will review your request and reply with availability, guidance, an estimated timescale and a quotation where possible.
